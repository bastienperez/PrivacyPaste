About PrivacyPaste

PrivacyPaste is a browser extension that protects your privacy by automatically anonymizing personally identifiable information (PII) when you paste text into AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity.

Technology: Microsoft Presidio

This extension is powered by Microsoft Presidio, an open-source solution developed by Microsoft for data anonymization and pseudonymization. Presidio uses advanced artificial intelligence and natural language processing techniques to identify and mask sensitive information.

Detection Capabilities

Presidio can detect and anonymize a wide range of personal information:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Social security numbers
  • Credit card numbers
  • Person names
  • IP addresses
  • And many other types of sensitive data
